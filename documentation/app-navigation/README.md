# App Navigation
### An outline of how a user would navigate through our application and the screens they would access. Our intended user is a new employee who has not created an account with the company. 

## 1. Sign In Screen
Our user will start on this page. They will be presented with two options, they can either enter their `email` and `password` to sign in, or click on the `Sign Up Button`. As our user has not created an account at this stage, the `Sign In Screen` will not be of much use to them, so the user's first step will be to click on the `Sign Up Button`. This will route them to the `Sign Up Screen`.

## 2. Sign Up Screen
Once the routing is complete, our user will land on the `Sign Up Screen`. Here they will be presented with four fields; `Full Name`, `Email`, `Password`, and `Confirm Password`. Our user will have the ability to fill in all of these fields. The `Password` field must meet specific requirements and the `Confirm Password` field must match the `Password` field. If the user does not meet these requirements when they click the `Submit Button` than the form will fail to submit. Form submission will route the user back to the `Sign In Screen`.

## 4. Sign In Screen
By entering their `Email` and `Password` and clicking the `Sign In Button`, our user will be navigated to the `Employee Information Screen`. 

## 5. Employee Information Screen
Here our user will be able to enter in their details into the empty fields. The `Employee Information Form` has (at minimum) 5 fields. Each field includes format validation, min/max validation. Some fields are required. Once the user has entered all of their details, they can click the `Submit Button` which will perfom some task.
